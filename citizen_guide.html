Citizen Connect / Citizen Guide

Citizen Guide

Learn how to easily navigate and use the digital services provided by EC Connect.

How to use CivicFeed

CivicFeed is your primary source for local news, announcements, and government updates.

  • Navigate to the CivicFeed page from the sidebar menu.
  • Scroll through the timeline to read real-time announcements posted by local administrators.

How to Book an Appointment

Skip the lines by scheduling your visit to the local government office in advance.

  • 1 Go to the Appointments page.
  • 2 Select the specific department you need to visit from the dropdown menu.
  • 3 Pick your preferred Date and Time, specify your purpose, and submit your request.

How to Request a Document

Apply for clearances, permits, and certificates directly from your phone.

  • 1 Go to the Request Doc page.
  • 2 Select the Document Type and state your purpose.
  • 3 Tap the upload area to attach up to 2 pictures of your Valid ID for verification.
  • 4 Review your details and submit. Track its progress on the right panel until it says "Ready for Pickup".

How to Report an Issue

Help maintain your community by reporting broken infrastructures or hazards.

  • 1 Go to the Report Issue page and type a description of the problem.
  • 2 Upload up to 5 photos as proof. (Tap any photo to enlarge it).
  • 3 Click Get Current Location to pin exactly where the issue is. Make sure your device GPS is turned ON.
  • 4 Submit and wait for action from local authorities. Track updates on the right panel.

How to Use Emergency Hotlines

Get instant access to rescue and police lines based on where you are.

  • Upon opening the Emergency page, the system will automatically scan your exact location.
  • Once your City or Municipality is detected, all local hotlines (Police, Fire, MDRRMO) will appear.
  • Tap the Phone Icon next to any number to dial it immediately on your phone.
